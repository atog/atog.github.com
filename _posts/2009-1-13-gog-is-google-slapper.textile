"A simple Google Slapper":http://gogglis.appspot.com/

bq. Ever felt like slapping someone with a google search, while in an IM conversation, chatrooms, or on Twitter? simply write http://gog.is and put the keywords in the url, like this: http://gog.is/clerks. This will redirect them to the google search for clerks. 

"I LIKE":http://gog.is/i/like. :)
