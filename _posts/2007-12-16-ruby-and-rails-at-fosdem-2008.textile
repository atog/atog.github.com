bq. The Belgian Ruby User Group is happy to announce the first Ruby and Rails devroom at Fosdem 2008. Fosdem is a gathering of Free and Open Source Software developers and the ideal occasion to meet fellow Ruby and Rails developers (and 2000 other open source developers). If you have a good idea for a talk or tutorial, consider sending in an abstract. You can find the Call for Papers at "http://wiki.rubyist.be/wiki/show/CallForPapers":http://wiki.rubyist.be/wiki/show/CallForPapers.
