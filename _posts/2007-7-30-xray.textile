"What is XRAY?":http://westciv.com/xray/

bq. XRAY is the first in hopefully a suite of free cross browser tools for helping web designers and developers better visualize what their code is doing in a browser. XRAY is designed to help you get beneath the skin of your web page.<br /><br/>XRAY let's you see the box model for any element on a page in action - where is the top and left of an element, how big is each margin, how big is the padding, how wide and high is the content box?

Ge moet het zelf maar is proberen. Het is knap gedaan mijn gedacht. Ik heb dezelfde funtionaliteit (ongeveer toch) al in "CSSEdit":http://www.macrabbit.com/cssedit/ maar om zoiets ook beschikbaar te hebben in een browser is, euhm, nifty! ;)
