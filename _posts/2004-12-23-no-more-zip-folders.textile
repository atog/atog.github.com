<a href="http://tortoisesvn.berlios.de/faq/index.php?action=artikel&cat=8&id=40&artlang=en">I just found out</a> you can disable zipfolders on Windows XP. Cool.

<code>regsvr32 /u zipfldr.dll</code>

To enable it again

<code>regsvr32 zipfldr.dll</code>

...and yes you have to reboot afterwards.
